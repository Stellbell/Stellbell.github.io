Wind Speed


As expected, the wind becomes stronger as one moves away from the equator (0 Deg. Latitude.) More interestingly, however, is the fact that the northern hemisphere tends to be windier this time of the year than the southern hemisphere. This may be due to the cloudiness at the time of the year this data was gathered.
